Hello, World

Welcome to Foundry — my little corner of the internet for projects, photography, and whatever else I feel like writing about.

Why I built this

I wanted somewhere to document things properly. GitHub repos are great for code, but they're a terrible place to explain why you made certain decisions, what you learned along the way, or just share something interesting you noticed while out with a camera.

This site is hand-built — no frameworks, no CMSs, just HTML, CSS, and a bit of JavaScript pulling things from GitHub. It felt right to keep it that way. Simple stacks are easier to reason about, and honestly more satisfying to work on.

What I'll write about

Expect a mix of:

  • Programming — Lua, Python, backend stuff, small experiments
  • Photography — gear thoughts, processing notes, maybe a few bird photos
  • Project deep-dives — the interesting decisions behind whatever I'm building

I'm not committing to a schedule. Posts will appear when there's something worth saying.

If you want to see what I'm working on right now, check out the Projects page — it pulls the latest commits live from GitHub.

Thanks for stopping by.
